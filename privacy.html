Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 18, 2026

YourKitchen ("we," "us," or "our") is operated by YourKitchen LLC, a Texas limited liability company. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information about you when you use our meal coordination platform at app.yourkitchen.app.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly when you create an account or use our services:

  • Name, email address, and phone number
  • Delivery address
  • Household size and dietary restrictions
  • Payment information (processed securely by Stripe — we do not store card details)
  • Restaurant preferences and favorite menu items

We also collect information automatically when you use our platform, including device type, browser type, IP address, and usage data.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Operate and improve the YourKitchen platform
  • Coordinate meal delivery between coordinators and recipients
  • Send SMS notifications about meal proposals, confirmations, and delivery status
  • Process payments through Stripe
  • Dispatch deliveries through DoorDash Drive
  • Respond to your questions and provide customer support
  • Send service-related communications

3. SMS Messaging

By providing your phone number and creating a Kitchen, you consent to receive SMS messages from YourKitchen. These messages include meal proposals, Y/N confirmation requests, delivery updates, and coordinator notifications. Message and data rates may apply. You can opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message. Reply HELP for help.

We do not sell or share your phone number with third parties for marketing purposes. Your number is used solely to operate the YourKitchen service.

4. Information Sharing

We share information only as necessary to operate our service:

  • Stripe — payment processing
  • DoorDash — delivery fulfillment (delivery address and order details)
  • Twilio — SMS delivery
  • Supabase — secure database hosting

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We do not share your information for advertising purposes.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information, including encrypted data storage, secure HTTPS connections, and access controls. Payment information is handled exclusively by Stripe and is never stored on our servers.

6. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us at marques@yourkitchen.app.

7. Children's Privacy

YourKitchen is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information. To exercise these rights, contact us at marques@yourkitchen.app. We will respond within 30 days.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by email or through the app. Continued use of YourKitchen after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
