For Organizations

Care, delivered
to the people you serve.

We're beginning to work with a handful of organizations who care for people at scale — getting warm, dignified meals to the doorsteps of the populations they serve. If that's your mission too, we'd love to talk.

Home health & aide organizations

Your aides already show up for clients who can't easily cook. YourKitchen lets you arrange reliable home-delivered meals around each client's needs — without adding to your team's load.

🥫

Food security nonprofits

For the families you serve, a hot meal delivered home with dignity is different from a pickup line. We help you reach food insecure households where they are.

🏥

Hospitals & care teams

Discharge is when support drops off. Coordinate post surgical and recovery meals for patients heading home — a simple, trackable extension of the care you already provide.

🤝

NGOs & community programs

Whatever population you serve — new parents, the grieving, the isolated, the recovering — we can stand up a meal coordination layer that fits how your community already works.
