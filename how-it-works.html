Common questions

The answers, in motion.

1

How do I set up a Kitchen?

Name it, set preferences, allergies, and a delivery window. About five minutes.

📹
Clip 1 · Set up a Kitchen
Create screen → fill details → land on the new calendar.
2

How does my village send a meal?

Pick a date, choose a meal from a local restaurant, leave a note, save your card.

📹
Clip 2 · Claim a date
Open calendar link → tap a date → pick restaurant → meal → note → save.
3

When does my card get charged?

Never before the recipient confirms. If they decline, you're not charged. Simple as that.

📹
Clip 3 · The charge
Card-save step lingering on "not charged until confirmed" copy.
4

What does the recipient actually see?

A simple text the day of: "Marques picked dinner for tonight — does this work?"

📹
Clip 4 · The text
The incoming SMS thread with the Y/N prompt.
5

What if I don't want that meal?

Reply N. You'll get options and can swap to something that sounds better.

📹
Clip 5 · The swap
Reply N → see options → swap to a different meal.
6

What happens after I confirm?

The order is placed for you and delivered to your door. Nothing else to do.

📹
Clip 6 · After confirm
Reply Y → confirmation → "on its way" / delivery state.
7

What's the village wall?

A shared space where your people leave notes and photos — and every meal shows up automatically.

📹
Clip 7 · Village wall
Open the cork board → posts/photos → auto-post on delivery.
